# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community.
Contributors should engage constructively, assume good intent, and critique
ideas rather than people.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and specific in technical discussion.
- Welcome questions and differing levels of experience.
- Protect private information and research participants.
- Correct mistakes transparently.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior
threatens the safety, privacy, or constructive operation of the community.
